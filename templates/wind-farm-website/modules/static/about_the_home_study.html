Home Study


This study will consist of 3 visit which will be separated by at least 11 days; each visit will consist of 4 consecutive days (i.e. 10am Friday to Noon Monday) where we will undertake a range of health-related measurements (sleep, brain activity, reaction time, memory, heart rate and blood samples etc. ) whilst in the background we will play 3 types of noise to you. The 3 types of noise are loud traffic noise which will probably interrupt your sleep, simulated wind turbine noise (infrasound - which you will not be able to hear) and silence.
